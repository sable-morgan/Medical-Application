House

HOUSE will be a medical diagnosis application. The application will deduce the 
time a patient is waiting for a medical diagnosis.The HOUSE application will take 
a patient’s symptoms, MRI, CT-scans, and X-ray images, along with lab results to 
predict the diagnosis of the patient. After diagnosis, the patient will receive 
suggested next steps for treatment or medical attention.
